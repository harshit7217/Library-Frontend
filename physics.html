By J.L. Meriam and L.G. Kraige
A comprehensive guide focused on the dynamics of particles and rigid bodies, commonly used in mechanical and civil engineering studies.

A book that introduces modern physics concepts such as quantum mechanics, relativity, and atomic structure, essential for advanced engineering applications.
